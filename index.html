關於我們

About Us

VORTEX (隊伍編號 34130) 是一支成立於 2025 年 9 月的 FTC 機器人隊伍，代表嗇色園主辦可譽中學暨可譽小學出戰。2025-2026 "Decode" 是我們的新秀賽季 (Rookie Year)，我們充滿熱情，準備好迎接挑戰！

Founded in September 2025, VORTEX (Team 34130) is a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team representing Hoyu College and Primary School (Sponsored by Sik Sik Yuen). The 2025-2026 "Decode" season is our rookie year, and we are geared up for the challenge!

首次友誼賽 (First Scrimmage)

First Scrimmage

在 2025 年 12 月，建隊不久的我們迎來了首次友誼賽！我們非常榮幸能與優秀隊伍 Beyonders (23138)、HOYU ARASAKA (25290)、HOYU Hephaestus (28818)、DGS Starburst (28784) 以及 Project R (11052) 以賽會友。這次交流不僅讓我們積累了寶貴的實戰經驗，交換了機器人設計心得，更讓我們深刻體會到 FTC 社區中互助分享的 "Gracious Professionalism" 精神。

In December 2025, shortly after our formation, we participated in our very first friendly scrimmage! We were incredibly honored to compete and exchange ideas with outstanding teams including Beyonders (23138), HOYU ARASAKA (25290), HOYU Hephaestus (28818), DGS Starburst (28784), and Project R (11052). This experience not only provided us with valuable on-field practice and insights into robot design, but also allowed us to deeply appreciate the spirit of "Gracious Professionalism" within the FTC community.

STEM 推廣與社區參與STEM Outreach

開放日 (Open Day)

Open Day

我們不僅致力於機器人比賽，更熱衷於推廣 STEM 教育。在學校的 開放日 期間，VORTEX 團隊與 HOYU ARASAKA 緊密合作，向同學們普及 STEM 概念，並負責對外講解及展示學校的 STEM LAB，激發更多人對科技的興趣。

Beyond robotics competitions, we are passionate about STEM education. During our school's Open Day, Team VORTEX partnered with HOYU ARASAKA to introduce STEM concepts to fellow students and guide visitors through the school's STEM LAB, inspiring the next generation of innovators.
